# Privacy Policy for MixGo Sonca – Sound FX

**Last Updated: April 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **MixGo Sonca** ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

### 1. Information We Access
Our App is designed to control audio mixer devices via Bluetooth. To provide this functionality, we request the following permissions:

*   **Bluetooth / Bluetooth Scan**: Required to search for and connect to your Sonca Mixer device.
*   **Location Services**: On Android, Bluetooth scanning requires access to your device's location. 

### 2. Information Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.**
*   **Location Data**: While the App requires location permissions to perform Bluetooth scans (a requirement of the Android operating system), **we do not collect, store, or transmit your physical location data** to any server.
*   **Personal Information**: We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers.
*   **Device Data**: We access Bluetooth device identifiers only to maintain a connection with your mixer. This data remains on your mobile device.

### 3. Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties. There are no advertising SDKs or third-party analytics trackers integrated into the App that collect your personal information.

### 4. Data Security
The security of your information is important to us. Since we do not collect or store data on external servers, the risk of data breaches involving your personal information is minimized. All communication between the App and your mixer device occurs locally over a secure Bluetooth connection.

### 5. Third-Party Services
Our App may contain links to external sites (such as our documentation). We have no control over the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

### 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
